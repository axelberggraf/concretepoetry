Concrete Poetry Drawing Tool

Get started:

a) Type in your own lines in the field above – click Add Line to add a line.
Type in the title of your poem/text and your name and click the Add Title and Add Name buttons respectively.

b) Find a random poem by chosing a line count (number of verses in the poem) and click the "Find Poem" button.

Draw in the canvas using your mouse/finger! If you are happy with your composition you can click the "Upload Image" button to upload it to the image gallery below.
